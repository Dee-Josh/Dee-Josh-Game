How to play

1 of 5

The goal of this game is click the box that the computer selects in the order it was selected.

On level 1, the computer selects a box and you are expected to click that box. Easy Peasy.

On level 2, the computer selects another box, you are expected to click. CLick the first box from level 1 followed by the box selected in level 2.

On level 3, the computer selects another box, you are expected to click the box from level 1 followed by the box selected in level 2 then the box just selected in level 3.
And on and on like that..

See how far you can go, very few people are able to reach level 15.
